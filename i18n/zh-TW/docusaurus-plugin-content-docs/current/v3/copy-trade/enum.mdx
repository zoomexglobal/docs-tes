### accountType
* `CONTRACT`&emsp;期貨帳戶
* `COPYTRADING`&emsp;跟單帳戶

### transferStatus
* `SUCCESS`
* `PENDING`
* `FAILED`

### symbol

> 跟單交易僅支持USDT永續

* `BTCUSDT`
* `ETHUSDT`
* `XRPUSDT`
* `LTCUSDT`
* `SOLUSDT`
* `...`

### orderType
* `Market`
* `Limit`

### side
* `Buy`
* `Sell`

### execType
* `Trade`
* `AdlTrade`
* `Funding`
* `BustTrade`

### triggerBy
* `LastPrice`
* `IndexPrice`
* `MarkPrice`

### lastLiquidityInd
* `AddedLiquidity`&emsp;增加流動性
* `RemovedLiquidity`&emsp;吃流動性
* `LiquidityIndNA`&emsp;不存在流動性變化

### copyTradeOrderStatus
* `UNKNOWN`
* `PendingNew`
* `New`
* `OpenOrderPartiallyFilled`
* `OpenOrderFilled`
* `OpenOrderClosing`
* `OpenOrderClosedFilled`
* `CloseOrderPartiallyFilled`
* `CloseOrderFilled`
* `Cancelled`
* `Untriggered`
* `Triggered`

### timeInForce
* `GoodTillCancel`
* `ImmediateOrCancel`
* `FillOrKill`
* `PostOnly`

### positionIdx
* `0`&emsp;單向持倉
* `1`&emsp;買側的雙向持倉
* `2`&emsp;賣側的雙向持倉

### interval
* `1` `3` `5` `15` `30` `60` `120` `240` `360` `720`&emsp;分鐘
* `D`&emsp;天
* `W`&emsp;週
* `M`&emsp;月

### tickDirection
* `PlusTick`&emsp;價格上漲
* `ZeroPlusTick`&emsp;交易的價格與前一筆交易的價格相同，前一筆交易的價格高於前一筆交易的價格
* `MinusTick`&emsp;價格下跌
* `ZeroMinusTick`&emsp;交易的價格與前一筆交易的價格相同，前一筆交易的價格低於前一筆交易的價格

### transferType
* `IN`&emsp;從主賬戶轉入到子賬戶
* `OUT`&emsp;從子賬戶轉出到主賬戶
